Plans and Goals

Currently I'm hoping to add even more things to my portfolio. Namely, a bunch of Jedi Academy/Movie Battles II mods that alot of them will be modelled by me personally. In which I'm actually aiming to join the development team that supports the MBII mod which is absolutely amazing. And amazingly has been withstanding against the stands of time. You can find out how that's going on this page in which is purely my content for MBII right here.

After that and learning how to make models, I wanna take it a step further and make my own Fallout/Skyrim mods, starting off with Fallout 4. Most notably I'm hoping to increase the variety of items in the world which will further add depth. It gets old running into two hundred year old Abraxo boxes... and once I make my presence in the Fallout/Skyrim modding community I'll be aiming to join one of the large development teams for Fallout expansions. Such as Fallout Miami, Cascadia, the Fallout One re-master, Capital Wasteland, New Vegas etc. I already have plans for what I'm going to do in extreme detail all laid out in a few Google Docs to help visualize and manage how this hopefully series of mods will turn out.
